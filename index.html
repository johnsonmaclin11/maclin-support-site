FAQ

Questions clients often ask before reaching out.

These answers help your site feel more complete today, and they reduce friction for future clients who want to know how to get started.

Can VertexOps support both operations and hiring at the same time?

Yes. VertexOps is positioned to support a mix of administrative execution, workflow coordination, and recruitment support depending on the client’s needs.

Will the website form send email right away?

The design is ready now. To make it send live submissions, connect the form later to Formspree, Netlify Forms, Zoho Forms, Zapier, or your own backend endpoint.

Can inquiry messages be routed to different inboxes later?

Yes. The service dropdown and field names are already structured so you can later route submissions by service type to contact, sales, support, or careers inboxes.

Can automated email replies be added later?

Yes. Once a live form backend is connected, you can trigger an auto-reply from the form service or from your email workflow. You can also set an email autoresponder directly in Hostinger for mailbox-based inquiries.
